## Q1 Graphics transformations (short_answer)
	
### Q1.1 Principle of transformations

The basic transformations are translation, rotation, and scaling, while composite transformations are just a sequence of basic transformations.

Homogeneous coordinates are used in transformation computing because it unifies the transformation formulas for both vectors and vertices. Allowing each transformation to be representable as a matrix, enhancing its simplicity and reliability.
